About Me

I think coding - and specifically software development - is the perfect combination of art, creativity, and problem solving. I have always been passionate about creativity and problem solving but it was not until the Covid-19 pandemic when I discovered my interest in coding.

One day, I took a JavaScript beginner course on The Odin Project. I was fascinated by how approachable and versatile the language was. A few more courses and some research later, I knew I wanted to pursue software development for my career. With my family's support, I quit my full-time job to accelerate my transition into software development. Over the course of the next few months, I finished the Full Stack JavaScript path on The Odin Project, got some certifications, worked on personal projects, joined a boot camp, worked on more personal projects, and applied to countless job opportunities before finally landing my first role as a software developer.

I currently work at Scholarships.com where I create and implement new designs, develop and optimize user flows, improve accessibility, improve site performance, refactor business logic, and conduct end-to-end testing. I also design, implement, and test email campaigns.

Previously, at United Healthcare, I developed and enhanced multiple blue chip features with high business impact and visibility. I also improved site reliability, performance, and accessibility.

Prior to working in software development, I led a team of manufacturing engineers at OSG making cutting tools for the aerospace, defense, and automotive industries. I received multiple promotions and formal recognition as a result of my commitment, accountability, and persistence to continuously improve myself, the products, the processes, and the people around me.
